## üí∞ **Financial Risk Analysis Framework**

### **üìä Single Loss Expectancy (SLE) Calculation Framework**

**SLE = Asset Value √ó Exposure Factor (EF)**

Where:
- **Asset Value:** Total value of affected business assets (revenue, data, systems)
- **Exposure Factor:** Percentage of asset value lost in a single incident (0.1-1.0)

**VaR = Impact (‚Ç¨) √ó Probability (decimal) √ó Confidence Factor √ó Time Horizon Factor**

- [ ] **Risk score calculation** follows formula: Probability √ó Impact √ó 100
